Opera invented Speed Dial in 2007 which is a great feature. A lot of people love it, but we are different: Alice likes bookmarks more than Speed Dial, Bob is a fan of minimalism and prefers blank start pages, and Eve uses her iGoogle page.

Are you one of them? Then try this extension and replace Speed Dial with your favorite start page.
